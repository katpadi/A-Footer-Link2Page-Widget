Footer to Link, Yo!

This is a swaggy simple WordPress widget that lets you add a link of an existing page to your footer. WHY? Because sometimes your clients want to add TONS and TONS of footer pages at random times. Never worry no more!!!

1. Download the zip file.
2. Drop it (like it's hot) into your plugins dir.
3. Activate!
